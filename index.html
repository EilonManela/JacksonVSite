Jackson King V

An iconic shape for the metal scene.

The King V is a famous guitar made by a company called Jackson Guitars, it's a pointy version of the Gibson Flying V which made it popular amongst many metal players, such as: Dave Mustaine of Megadeath, James Hetfield of Metallica, Scott Ian of Anthrax and Wes Borland of Limp Bizkit.

King V was named after Robbin Crosby from the 80's band Ratt, which was nicknamed "King" because of his unusual height, being 6'6 tall.
